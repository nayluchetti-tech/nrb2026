Select the mode that fits the moment

The tool has 3 capture modes. Pick the one that makes sense for the situation:

🎤 Audio Note — Best after a conversation. Record a quick voice note summarizing who you talked to and what they said. The AI will transcribe it and fill in the form fields.

📷 Photo Capture — Snap a photo of their business card or NRB badge. The tool will try to OCR the text and auto-fill name, email, company, and website.

✍ Type It In — Manually enter the lead's details. Use this when you have their info from a conversation, email, or written note.

Pro tip: You can combine modes! After recording audio, you can also attach a card photo. After snapping a photo, you can add more details in the form. All modes lead to the same form.

Quick Reference & Tips

Best for Quick Captures

Photo mode → snap the card → "Send to Google Sheets." Takes 10 seconds. Add a quick note for bonus points.

Best for Detailed Leads

Audio mode after the conversation. Include name, company, product interest, pain points, and your gut feeling rating.

When to Use Type It In

When someone tells you their email verbally, or you're entering leads from written notes at the end of the day.

QUALIFIED Lead Rule

If they distribute via Broadcast TV, Radio, or Satellite (or spend $60K+/yr on marketing) → they're QUALIFIED. Open "Qualification Details" and check those boxes.

OCR from Business Cards

The tool tries to auto-read email, name, company, and website from card photos. It works best with clean, printed cards in good lighting.

All Fields Are Optional

Don't let the form slow you down. Email + name + a note is enough. You can always enrich the data later. Speed matters more at the booth.

Products to Select

PRAY Radio, PRAY TV, PRAYStudio Tools, PRAY AI Tools, SuperFunnel, PRAY CaaS, Advertise on PRAY, Partnership with PRAY

Meeting Quality Scale

1 = Long shot | 2 = Lukewarm | 3 = Warm opportunity | 4 = Strong interest | 5 = Ready to close

Remember: The goal is to capture every interaction, even brief ones. A business card photo with zero form fields filled out is still better than nothing. The marketing team can enrich the data later — but only if the lead is captured.
